Self-Storage Management Software: How MoreSpace Ibiza Automates Billing with Storagefy

MoreSpace Ibiza uses Storagefy, a self-storage and parking management software, to automate recurring payments and operations. We manage 32 storage containers in San Rafael (Ibiza) from a single platform.

Facility context

  • 32 storage containers
  • Location: Calle des Llaudadors 13, San Rafael (Ibiza)
  • Facility type: Container storage (maritime containers)
  • Audience: Individuals, freelancers and businesses in Ibiza

The challenge: manual billing and administrative workload

Before implementing an automated system, running a self-storage business meant a considerable administrative burden. Manual billing creates recurring problems in the sector:

  • Manual collections: reminders sent by hand, tracking in Excel and risk of oversights.
  • Payment tracking complexity: knowing who has paid and who hasn't requires time and dedication.
  • Lack of centralised management: bookings, contracts and invoices scattered across spreadsheets and emails.
  • High late payment rates: when collections are manual, late payments in storage often exceed 30%.
  • Limited scalability: adding more units means multiplying the administrative load.

In Ibiza, where seasonality and the diversity of customers (residents, seasonal, businesses) make management even more complex, we needed a solution that would centralise everything and reduce manual work.

The solution: Storagefy to automate operations

Storagefy is a SaaS management software for self-storage and parking operators. It allows you to automate recurring payments with Stripe (card or SEPA Direct Debit), configure monthly, bi-weekly, quarterly or semi-annual billing cycles, and centralise bookings, access control and invoicing in a single system. We've used it at MoreSpace from day one.

This is how we use it in practice:

  • Recurring payment setup: we define the cycle (monthly, quarterly, etc.) and the customer enters their details once.
  • Stripe integration: automatic charges by card or SEPA direct debit, with no manual intervention.
  • Automatic charge on date: the system attempts the charge on the scheduled date; if it fails, it sends automatic reminders.
  • Dashboard visibility: we see the status of each unit, pending payments and occupancy in real time.
  • Unit and customer management: online bookings, customer area and centralised invoicing.

The results: less manual work, better control

After implementing Storagefy, the operational flow changed significantly:

  • Reduced manual work: we no longer send reminders by hand or update spreadsheets.
  • Better payment consistency: recurring charges and automatic reminders reduce late payments.
  • Clearer financial control: visibility of charges, invoices and status of each container from a single panel.
  • Easier scalability: adding more units doesn't mean multiplying the administrative load.
  • Improved customer experience: booking in 2 minutes, customer area for invoices and 24/7 payments.

Automation isn't just for us. Our customers enjoy real-time online booking, invoices always available and total price transparency.

How recurring billing works in practice

This is how automatic collection works in our day-to-day:

1. Payment cycle setup

We define the cycle (monthly, quarterly, etc.) and payment method. The customer enters their details once.

2. Automatic charge on date

The system attempts the charge on the scheduled date. If it fails, it sends automatic reminders.

3. Invoice and access without friction

The customer receives the invoice by email and maintains access to their container. No paperwork or calls.

Common mistakes with manual billing in storage

Many storage operators make these mistakes when collecting manually:

  • × Sending reminders late or inconsistently, which increases late payments
  • × Not having 24/7 payment links — the customer wants to pay but can't outside office hours
  • × Wasting time with Excel and spreadsheets that get out of date and cause errors
  • × Not integrating payments with access control — late payments without automatic blocking

Frequently asked questions about storage automation

How to automate recurring payments in storage?

You can automate recurring payments using management software like Storagefy integrated with Stripe. The customer enters their payment details once and the system charges automatically each cycle (monthly, quarterly, etc.). Includes reminders and 24/7 payment links.

What invoicing software to use for self storage?

For self storage, software that integrates bookings, recurring payments, access control and invoicing is ideal. Platforms like Storagefy offer everything in one with Stripe for card and SEPA Direct Debit, configurable cycles and customer area.

How does automatic payment with Stripe work in storage?

Stripe allows recurring payments through subscription. The operator configures the billing cycle and the customer saves their card or SEPA mandate. Stripe attempts the charge each cycle and manages retries and reminders automatically.

What is SEPA recurring billing for storage?

SEPA recurring billing uses bank direct debit to charge rent automatically each month or in the agreed cycle. The customer authorises the charge once and the bank executes subsequent charges without manual intervention.
